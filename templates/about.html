DAN OCHIENG & CO. ADVOCATES is one of the premier law firms in Kenya, with office in Kisumu, we are a medium size but highly professional full-service firm: specializing in: Land Law, Commercial Law, Company Law, Property, Conveyance, Trusts, Family and Succession Law, Insurance (Running Down), Banking and Securities Law, Employment Law, Civil Litigation, Environmental Law, Legal Research and Consultancy,

The law firm combines in-depth knowledge and vast expertise to uphold the most excellent standards of legal practice. We focus on a wide range of intricate legal issues, opening new horizons and fresh perspectives for our clients. Small but highly professional, our law firm is configured to deliver the highest class service to our clients, in spite of the difficulty of the legal problem.

As a top Kenyan law firm, we are very proud of our strong working relationships with major law firms around the country. We also provide professional services to well- known corporates that rely on our competence to represent and manage their interests in Kenya.

Our ongoing success and strong reputation derives from a highly regarded team of 3 lawyers who combine intellectual capability and unique practical skills.

The firm took over the cases from the firm of Ochieng & Associates Advocates, which has been in practice for over five years.

Our Bussines Principles & Approach

This firm’s Business Policy, binds all members of staff and demands ethical conduct in their daily relationships with colleagues or interactions with clients. The company staff must:

How we work with clients to achieve successful outcomes

  • 1. Perform duties with honesty, integrity, and to the best of their abilities.
  • 2. Maintain impartiality and avoid compromising their professional positions.
  • 3. Treat clients with fairness, courtesy, dignity, and sensitivity to their rights.
  • 4. Remain accountable for actions and accept constructive criticism for improvement.
  • 5. Comply with all company procedures and uphold its positive image at all times.
  • 6. Use company information only for its intended purpose, within delegated authority.
  • 7. Respect and care for the company's assets and property as their own.
  • 8. Disclose any personal or company-related conflicts of interest to avoid compromise.
  • 9. Decline any gifts that may be perceived as attempts to exert undue influence.
  • 10. Report behavior that violates the code, while encouraging constructive feedback and contributions.
  • 11. Seek guidance from supervisors or the managing partner when uncertain about policy application.

We are committed to these principles and strive to exceed our clients' expectations in every interaction.
