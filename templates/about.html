DAN OCHIENG & CO. ADVOCATES is one of the premier law firms in Kenya, with office in Kisumu. We are a medium size but highly professional full-service firm specializing in land law, commercial law, company law, property and conveyance, trusts, family and succession law, insurance, banking and securities law, employment law, civil litigation, environmental law, and legal research and consultancy.

The law firm combines in-depth knowledge and vast expertise to uphold the most excellent standards of legal practice. Small but highly professional, our law firm is configured to deliver the highest class service to our clients.

Our Business Principles & Approach

This firm's Business Policy binds all members of staff and demands ethical conduct in their daily relationships.

  1. Perform duties with honesty, integrity, and to the best of their abilities.
  2. Maintain impartiality and avoid compromising their professional positions.
  3. Treat clients with fairness, courtesy, dignity, and sensitivity to their rights.
  4. Remain accountable for actions and accept constructive criticism for improvement.
  5. Comply with all company procedures and uphold its positive image at all times.
  6. Use company information only for its intended purpose, within delegated authority.
  7. Respect and care for the company's assets and property as their own.
  8. Disclose any personal or company-related conflicts of interest.
  9. Decline any gifts that may be perceived as attempts to exert undue influence.
  10. Report behavior that violates the code, while encouraging constructive feedback.
  11. Seek guidance from supervisors or the managing partner when uncertain about policy application.
